Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 07, 2025

Thank you for using Focus Timer: Distraction Counter ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information that can identify you, such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Apple ID or payment details

b. Non-Personal & Usage Data

The App collects minimal, anonymous usage data stored locally on your device only:

  • Focus session duration
  • Number of distractions per session
  • Timestamps of completed sessions
  • Reminder preferences (delay, quiet hours)

This data never leaves your device and is used only to display your history and analytics within the App.

c. In-App Purchases

Premium features are purchased via Apple's In-App Purchase system. We do not receive or store:

  • Payment information
  • Receipts
  • Transaction details

All purchases are processed and managed securely by Apple. Your purchase status is verified locally using Apple's receipt validation (no server involved).

2. How We Use Your Information

The App uses locally stored data to:

  • Display your focus history
  • Show analytics (total focus time, best day, etc.)
  • Schedule optional reminder notifications
  • Unlock Premium features after purchase

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored locally on your iPhone using secure, encrypted iOS storage.
  • No data is transmitted to any server.
  • No third-party analytics or tracking tools are used.

4. Notifications

With your permission, the App may send local notifications to:

  • Remind you to resume a paused focus session
  • Notify you when a timer completes

You can disable notifications anytime in iPhone Settings → Focus Timer.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You will be notified of any changes within the App. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
